## How It Started

It began with a shared booth at a crowded farmers market and a disagreement about the best way to eat a peach. Two years later, here we are.

We met in the summer of 2022, fell in love over long walks, bad movies, and an embarrassing number of trips to the same taco truck. We got engaged on a hiking trail in October 2024, with no fanfare — just the two of us, a sweeping view, and a ring hidden in a jacket pocket for three nervous miles.

## Why December

December in South Texas feels like the world finally exhaling. The heat softens, the evenings cool, and the sky goes wide and clear. It felt like the right place and the right time to start something new.

We're so grateful you're part of this chapter.

— **Jamal & Gabriela**
