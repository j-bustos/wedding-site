## The Reception

Following the ceremony, join us for an evening of celebration, good food, and dancing under the stars.

**Time:** 6:30 PM – 11:00 PM  
**Location:** Los Encinos  
McAllen, TX

The reception will begin with cocktails in the garden before moving into the main hall for dinner. Expect a family-style feast featuring local South Texas flavors, wood-fired meats, and hand-crafted desserts.

The evening will include toasts, first dances, and a live band playing into the night.
